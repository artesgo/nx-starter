How to use this app

This is an app that projects your budget into the future. There's some fiddling involved, but at least the recurring expense and incomes are mostly handled for you.

Calendar

Use the calendar to select dates, I think.. I don't remember.. try not to pick dates in the past, this app is more for future planning. Past dated items will be consolidated. (They are removed and grouped into the Todays balance item)

The Spicy Details

Description
Use the description and amount fields to add a budget item
 Amount
Positive amounts for income, negative amounts for expenses

Recurring Items

Use the radio buttons to select how the item should recurr
None
None means one-time income or expense
 Weekly
Weekly is uhhh... every week
 Bi-Weekly
Bi-Weekly is every two weeks
 Monthly
Monthly for once a month items
 Yearly
Yearly if you've signed up for those yearly discounts

The Clicky Parts

Budget creates the item based on all of the previous fields
Recur to 6 months will look for recurring items in your budget and project them out 6 months
You have to click the recur button every once in a while to update your data
It uses the last occurrence of a recurring item to calculate the next one
If you get done paying the government CPP taxes, you can add another Salary item with the updated amount instead of using Recur
You may also have to delete some of the recurring items that aren't accurate and then run Recur again
Buy me a Ko-fi will take you to my Ko-fi page, if you want... it's not like I want you to buy me a coffee or anything.

The Table

The table is a list of all of your budget items. You can edit the amount, manually recurr or delete the item.
The app will automatically consolidate budget items that occur in the past into the current day. This of course can get out of date over time, but it's a simple update to Todays Balance (usually the first item) in the table.
You will also have recurring expenses that you don't know the final total. Credit card debt is one of those things. Just add a recurring estimate and you can come back and update in the table as you go.
There's also a search that will filter the table by description so you can edit a specific category quickly.

The Cityscape

This is a quick overview of your next 6 months. You can hover over the bars to see the estimated balance based on the budget items you've entered.
