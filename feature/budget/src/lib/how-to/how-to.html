How to use this app

Calendar

Use the calendar to select dates, i think

The Spicy Details

Description
Use the description and amount fields to add a budget item
Amount
Positive amounts for income, negative amounts for expenses

Recurring Items

Use the radio buttons to select how the item should recurr
None
None means one-time income or expense
Weekly
Weekly is uhhh... every week
Bi-Weekly
Bi-Weekly is every two weeks
Monthly
Monthly for once a month items
Yearly
Yearly if you've signed up for those yearly discounts

The Clicky Parts

Budget creates the item based on all of the previous fields
Recur to 6 months will look for recurring items in your budget and project them out 6 months
You have to click the recur button every once in a while to update your data
It uses the last occurrence of a recurring item to calculate the next one
If you get done paying the government CPP taxes, you can add another Salary item with the updated amount instead of using Recur
You may also have to delete some of the recurring items that aren't accurate and then run Recur again
Buy me a Ko-fi will take you to my Ko-fi page, if you want... it's not like I want you to buy me a coffee or anything.

Why...

I wanted an app that can somewhat keep track of my finances without too much fiddling. There's still some fiddling, but at least the recurring expense and incomes are handled for you.
The app will automatically consolidate budget items that occur in the past into the current day. This of course can get out of date over time, so just delete it and re-enter the value with today's date as a non-recurring item. This is done to keep the number of bars in the barchart consistent and your data from bloating the browser storage.
